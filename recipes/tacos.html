Tacos

Description

These tacos were... SPICY. They were so spicy that I straight up had to throw my contacts out from being in the proximity of them. The flavor though.... mmmph.

This dish is really nothing too complicated, just some Mexican staples and a whole lot of love. Would definitely recommend these to go with a bag of blue corn tortilla chips, some Coronas and a good Kitboga video.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium corn tortillas
  • 1 cup rice *optional
  • 1 lb of chicken tenders
  • 1/4 lb Monterey Jack cheese
  • 2 small bell peppers
  • 1 1/2 med avocados
  • 1/2 med onion
  • 2 med cloves of garlic
  • 3 serano peppers
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook rice in your preferred method (ie rice cooker, pot, etc)
  2. Wash & chop veggies
  3. Mash avocados and mix with half the onion, garlic & lemon
  4. Cut the chicken tenders in half, length-wise
  5. Saute tenders, season with salt & pepper
  6. In a separate pan saute peppers & onion
  7. Heat up tortillas on a small pan with a touch of oil, place slices of cheese on top of tortillas and allow to melt slightly
  8. Serve by placing tortillas on plate, then chicken and veggie mixture, top with guacamole
